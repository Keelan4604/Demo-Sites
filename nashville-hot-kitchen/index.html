FAQ

Good to know.

Is there parking? ▼

Yes. Free lot in front, plus overflow street parking on the side. Valet on weekends after 6pm.

Do you handle dietary restrictions? ▼

Absolutely. Most mains can be made gluten-free, dairy-free, or vegetarian. Tell your server, the kitchen will accommodate.

Can I book a private event? ▼

Yes. We have a private dining room that seats 30 and a patio that fits 60. Email events@ for the package.

Do you sell gift cards? ▼

Physical and digital, any amount $25 and up. Buy at the host stand or through our online ordering page.

Do you take reservations? ▼

Yes for parties of 4 or more. Walk-ins welcome any time. Use the form below or call us direct.

How long is takeout right now? ▼

Average prep time is 15 to 25 minutes. The online order confirmation will give you a live ETA.

Is there a kids menu? ▼

Yes. Burger, chicken tenders, mac and cheese, or pasta with butter. All $7 with a side and drink.

How do you handle allergies? ▼

Tell your server when you order. We re-clean prep surfaces and switch gloves. Full allergen sheet available on request.
