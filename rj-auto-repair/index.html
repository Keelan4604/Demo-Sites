Not the Biggest Shop. Just the Most Trusted.

R&J Auto Repair was founded by John right here in Ypsilanti over two decades ago. With 30+ years of hands-on mechanic experience, John built this shop on a simple promise: do honest work, charge fair prices, and treat every car like it's family.

We're proud to be a family-owned business serving the Ypsilanti, Ann Arbor, and greater Washtenaw County community. From University of Michigan students needing a quick fix to families trusting us with their daily drivers — we take care of our neighbors.
