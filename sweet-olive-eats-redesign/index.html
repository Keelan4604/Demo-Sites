Our Story

About Sweet Olive

Sweet Olive Eats started with a simple dream: bring honest, farm-to-table food to the streets of Tuscaloosa. What began as a passion project at local farmers markets has grown into one of the most beloved food trucks in West Alabama.

We source our ingredients from local farms whenever possible, building relationships with the people who grow our food. Every recipe is crafted to honor Southern tradition while bringing something fresh and unexpected to the table.

From wedding receptions under the oaks to game day tailgates at Bryant-Denny, we've had the privilege of being part of thousands of special moments. Your event deserves food that people remember.
