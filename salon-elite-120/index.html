FAQ

Good to know.

What's your cancellation policy? ▼

We ask for 24 hours notice on cancellations or reschedules. Inside 24 hours runs a 50% service charge, no-shows are 100%. We'll always work with you on emergencies.

What products do you use? ▼

Olaplex, K18, Redken, Wella, Schwarzkopf, and a few smaller indie color lines. Everything we use is also available for retail in-house.

Do you do wedding / event packages? ▼

Yes. We bundle bridal trial, day-of styling, and bridesmaid services. On-site travel available within 30 miles. Email us for the full package.

Are kids welcome? ▼

Yes. Kids cuts are $25 and under. We ask that very young kids come with one parent for safety.

Do you take walk-ins? ▼

Whenever a stylist has openings. Booking ahead is the only way to guarantee your slot, especially Friday and Saturday.

What payment methods do you take? ▼

All major cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, cash. Tips are appreciated and can be added to card. Payment plans on jobs over $400 via Square Afterpay.

Can you install or remove extensions? ▼

Yes to both. New install includes consult, color match, and 4 to 8 week move-up included for the first cycle. Removal is $75 to $150.

Do you do color correction? ▼

Absolutely. Color correction is by consult only (free, 20 minutes) so we can quote accurately. Most corrections run 2 to 5 hours and $250 to $600.
