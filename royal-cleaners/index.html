FAQ

Quick answers.

Do you bring your own supplies? ▼

Yes. Pro-grade eco-friendly products, microfiber cloths, vacuums, and mops. We swap fresh cloths between rooms (no cross-contamination).

What if I have pets? ▼

We love them. Just let us know in your booking notes. We'll secure them or work around them based on your preference.

How do you handle keys? ▼

Coded lockboxes, smart-lock codes, or hand-off in person. Whatever works for you. Keys are tagged by code only, never with your address.

What's your cancellation policy? ▼

Free cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your visit. Inside 24 hours we charge 50% of the visit fee to cover the crew.

Do recurring clients get a discount? ▼

Yes. Weekly clients save 20% off the one-time rate, bi-weekly save 15%, monthly save 10%. Locked in at signup.

What's included in a standard clean? ▼

Dust all surfaces, vacuum + mop all floors, full kitchen wipe-down, all bathrooms scrubbed and sanitized, beds made (sheets you leave out), trash out. Detailed checklist in your booking confirmation.

Can I add special requests? ▼

Always. Inside fridge, inside oven, organize one closet, hand-wash dishes, water plants. Add-ons are clearly priced in the booking flow.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee? ▼

Yes. If something is missed or not up to standard, call us within 24 hours and we re-clean the space at no charge. No arguments.
