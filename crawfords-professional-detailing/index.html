FAQ

Common questions.

What kind of warranty do you offer? ▼

Every repair carries a 24-month / 24,000-mile nationwide warranty through TechNet. If something we touched fails inside that window, we fix it free at any partner shop in North America.

What payment methods do you take? ▼

Cash, all major cards, ACH, fleet cards, and CarCareONE financing. We invoice digitally so you have a paper trail.

Do you offer financing? ▼

Yes. CarCareONE and Sunbit, both with soft credit pulls and instant decisions. 6 to 60 month terms. We can get most repairs over $300 financed in under 5 minutes at the counter.

Do you use OEM parts? ▼

Your call. We carry both OEM and high-grade aftermarket. Quote shows both options with the price difference so you can decide what fits your budget.

Can you arrange towing? ▼

Yes. We have a tow partner that can pick your car up anywhere in the metro for a flat fee that gets credited toward your repair if you book the work with us.

Do you offer loaner cars? ▼

Loaners available on jobs over $1,000 or when a repair runs more than 2 days. First-come, first-served. We also Uber-credit you for shorter stays.

What about after-hours drop-off? ▼

Yes. Locked drop-box at the front door with envelopes. Drop the keys, fill out the slip, we'll call you in the morning before any work starts.

Is there a diagnostic fee? ▼

Flat $89 diagnostic. Fully waived if you book the repair with us the same visit. No mystery hourly clock running while we look.
