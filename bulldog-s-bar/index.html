FAQ

Before you head over.

Do you take reservations? ▼

We hold tables for parties of 6 or more, Tuesday through Thursday. Friday and Saturday are first-come, first-served at the bar.

Is there a dress code? ▼

No formal dress code. We ask that you keep it clean and respectful. No offensive graphics, no athletic shorts after 9pm on weekends.

Can you handle large groups? ▼

Yes. Our back room seats 40 and our patio fits another 30. Use the Private Events form below for groups of 12 or more.

Do you serve food? ▼

Full bar food menu until close: shareables, burgers, wings, flatbreads. Kitchen never closes before last call.

When is the live music? ▼

Thursdays from 9pm, plus most Friday DJ sets at 10pm. Follow our Instagram for special acts and ticketed nights.

Where do I park? ▼

Two-hour street parking on the block, plus a paid lot half a block south. We recommend rideshare for late nights, drop-off zone is right out front.

When is last call? ▼

Mon-Thu last call is 11:30pm. Fri-Sat last call is 1:30am. Sunday last call is 9:30pm.

When does the kitchen close? ▼

Kitchen closes 30 minutes before last call every night. Order your late-night fries before then or you'll regret it.
