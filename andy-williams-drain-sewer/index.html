EDUCATION

Common signs you need a plumber.

If you spot any of these, don't wait. Small problems become floods. Call us for a free phone consult.

⚠ Sudden spike in your water bill

A hidden leak under your slab or in a wall can waste hundreds of gallons before you ever see a wet spot.

⚠ Brown or rust-colored water

Usually a corroded water heater or galvanized pipe. Catch it early and you avoid a full repipe.

⚠ Drains slow in multiple fixtures

When more than one drain is sluggish, the problem is usually downstream in your main line.

⚠ Gurgling sounds from toilets

Air being pulled through a clogged or partially blocked sewer vent. Get it inspected before it backs up.

⚠ Wet spots in the yard or driveway

Strong sign of a buried supply or sewer line leak. Trenchless repair can fix it without digging up landscaping.

⚠ Low water pressure throughout the home

Could be a pressure regulator failure, a hidden leak, or aging galvanized pipe restricting flow.
