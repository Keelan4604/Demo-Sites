FAQ

Quick answers before you call.

How fast can you get to my home for an emergency? ▼

Within Brookwood we average a 60 to 90 minute arrival window for true no-heat / no-cool calls, 24/7.

Do you charge a service call fee? ▼

Yes, a flat diagnostic fee that gets fully waived if you book the repair with us the same visit.

Are your technicians certified? ▼

Every lead tech is NATE-certified, EPA 608 universal, and background-checked. We pull permits on every install.

What financing do you offer? ▼

0% APR for 12 months on qualifying systems, plus longer 60 and 84 month plans through our finance partners. Soft credit pull, instant decision.

How long does a new system install take? ▼

Most full system replacements are done in a single day. Larger jobs with new ductwork run 2 to 3 days.

What brands do you install? ▼

Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Mitsubishi, Bosch and Daikin. We pick the right brand for your budget and home, not the one with the highest spiff.

Do you offer rebates? ▼

Yes. We file all utility and federal IRA rebates for you. Heat pump installs can qualify for up to $8,000 in stacked rebates.

What is included in the maintenance plan? ▼

Two seasonal tune-ups per year, 15% off any repairs, no overtime fees, and priority dispatch ahead of non-members.
