FAQ

Quick answers.

How often should I mow? ▼

Most lawns in Northport, AL are weekly during peak growing season (April through October), bi-weekly otherwise. We'll set a frequency that matches your grass type and sun exposure.

How do I pay? ▼

Auto-pay on a saved card after each visit, or monthly invoicing for recurring contracts. We accept all major cards, ACH, and checks.

What if it rains on my service day? ▼

We push the visit to the next dry day, no extra charge. Your account stays on schedule and you don't pay for a skipped visit.

Do you bring your own equipment? ▼

Always. Commercial-grade mowers, blowers, trimmers. Sharpened blades every two weeks for clean cuts that don't tear your grass.

Do you offer organic or natural lawn care? ▼

Yes. We have an all-organic program (compost tea, chicken-meal fertilizer, corn gluten pre-emergent) for clients who want it. Pricing is about 15% more than conventional.

Do you do irrigation tune-ups? ▼

Yes. Spring start-up and fall blowout are our most common, but we also handle leaks, valve replacements, and full controller upgrades.

Can you handle a hardscape consult? ▼

Absolutely. We design and build pavers, retaining walls, fire pits, and outdoor kitchens in-house. Free on-site consult and a detailed proposal within 5 days.

Do you do commercial accounts? ▼

Yes. We service HOAs, office parks, and retail centers across Northport, AL. Volume contracts and full insurance on file. Use the form below or call for a walk-through.
