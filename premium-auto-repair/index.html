Our Story
Three Decades of Trust,
One Wrench at a Time

What started as one mechanic's dream in 1994 has grown into Nashville's most recommended independent shop. Here's how we got here.

1994
Kevin Furness opens Premium Auto Repair with a single bay, a toolbox, and a commitment to honest work. Word spreads fast through Nashville's tight-knit neighborhoods.
2002
Expansion time -- new bays, advanced diagnostic equipment, and a growing team of ASE-certified technicians to keep up with Nashville's booming population.
2010
Becomes one of the first independent shops in Middle Tennessee to invest in hybrid and modern vehicle diagnostic systems. Always ahead of the curve.
2024
30 years strong. A 96% recommendation rate on Facebook. Tens of thousands of vehicles repaired. The same values from day one: honesty, quality, and fair pricing.
What We Do
Full-Service Auto Repair

From routine maintenance to major repairs -- if it has an engine, we can fix it. Every service backed by our 30-year reputation.

Brake Service

Pads, rotors, calipers, fluid flushes. Complete brake system inspection and repair to keep you stopping safely.

Engine Repair

From minor tune-ups to complete engine overhauls. Expert diagnosis and repair of all engine problems, foreign and domestic.

Transmission

Automatic and manual transmission service, rebuilds, and fluid exchange. Smooth shifting restored by experienced hands.

Diagnostics

State-of-the-art computerized diagnostics. We find the real problem, not just the symptoms -- saving you time and money.

AC & Heating

Nashville summers are brutal. Full climate system service -- compressor, condenser, evaporator, heater core, and recharges.

Oil Changes

Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic. Quick service with quality filters -- the foundation of engine longevity.

Tune-Ups

Spark plugs, ignition systems, fuel system cleaning. Restore your engine's peak performance and fuel efficiency.

Suspension

Shocks, struts, springs, control arms, and alignments. Smooth ride restoration for Nashville's roads and potholes.

Exhaust Systems

Mufflers, catalytic converters, exhaust pipes, and manifolds. Quiet your ride and pass emissions with confidence.
