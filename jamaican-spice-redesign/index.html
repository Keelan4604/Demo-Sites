Our Story

Bringing the Heart of Jamaica to Michigan

At Jamaican Spice, every meal is a celebration of our heritage. We use authentic recipes passed down through generations, prepared with the freshest ingredients and the same passion that fills kitchens across Kingston and Montego Bay.

From our signature jerk seasoning - slow-smoked over pimento wood - to our rich, tender oxtail braised for hours, we pour love into every plate. We are proud to be Ypsilanti's home for real Jamaican cooking, and a beloved gathering place for the community.
