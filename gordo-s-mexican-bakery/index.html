MEET THE ROASTER

From farm gate to your cup.

Our beans come from a small roastery a short drive from Homewood, AL, run by a husband-and-wife team who source direct from farms in Ethiopia, Guatemala, and Colombia. They roast in small batches twice a week, so the beans you sip today were green coffee beans seven days ago.

No middlemen. No mystery blends. Every bag tells you the farm, the elevation, the roast date, and the cupping notes. We pick the lots that taste like a place, and we change the menu when the seasons change.

FAQ

Good to know.

Do you have oat milk and other non-dairy options? ▼

Yes. Oat, almond, soy, and coconut on the bar at no extra charge. House oat is our default for matcha.

Do you serve decaf? ▼

Yes, single-origin decaffeinated by the Swiss Water process. Available for any espresso drink or as drip on request.

How do you handle allergens? ▼

We bake in a shared kitchen but mark gluten-free, vegan, and nut-containing items on the case. Tell your barista about severe allergies.

What's the WiFi password? ▼

It changes monthly and is posted on a small chalkboard by the bar. Ask any team member.

Are laptops welcome all day? ▼

Anytime except Saturday and Sunday from 9am to noon, when we ask laptop users to wrap up so seating opens for our brunch crowd.

Do you cater meetings or events? ▼

Yes. Coffee boxes, pastry trays, and full barista bar service. 48-hour notice preferred. Use the form below or call us.

Do you sell gift cards? ▼

Physical and digital, in any amount $10 and up. Buy at the register or through the order form.

Is your patio dog friendly? ▼

Absolutely. The patio is leash-friendly and we keep a water bowl by the door. Treats live behind the espresso machine.
