FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Do you take my insurance? ▼

We're in-network with most major carriers (see the list above) and submit out-of-network claims as a courtesy. Call our front desk with your member ID for an exact verification.

What happens at my first visit? ▼

A new-patient exam includes digital X-rays, an oral cancer screening, a full periodontal assessment, and a meeting with the doctor to discuss any findings. Plan on about 75 minutes.

Do you offer payment plans? ▼

Yes. We accept CareCredit (0% APR for 6, 12, and 18 months on qualifying treatment) and offer in-house financing on plans over $1,500.

What if I have a dental emergency? ▼

Call our main line. We hold same-day slots every weekday for true emergencies (pain, swelling, broken teeth, lost crowns). After hours, leave a voicemail and the on-call doctor will return within 60 minutes.

Do you treat kids? ▼

Yes. Dr. Reyes is a board-certified pediatric specialist. We see kids from age 1 (first tooth) through their teenage years.

Do you offer sedation? ▼

Yes. Nitrous oxide for mild anxiety, oral conscious sedation for moderate, and IV sedation for full surgical procedures. Discussed at your consult.

Can I get a second opinion? ▼

Always. Bring your X-rays and treatment plan. We'll review and tell you honestly what we'd do, even if that's nothing. No pressure.

Is parking easy? ▼

Free patient lot directly out front, plus an accessible entrance and elevator if you need them. We validate any street parking too.
